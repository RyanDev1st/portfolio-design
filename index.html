About

        
I build the harness that makes models behave in production.

        
Most AI demos work once. The hard part is the part nobody sees — the loop around the model: tool policy, prompt evaluation, routing, and the recovery path for when a step goes wrong. That scaffolding is what I design, and it is what turns a clever prototype into a system you can ship and trust.
