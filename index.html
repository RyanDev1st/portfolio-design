System Design

              
FORGER, gated end to end

              
FORGER turned one-off glue into an execution-gated harness. Its six letters are its phases.

              

                
Contract . phase 0Write the Definition of Works, the single source of truth every gate downstream checks against.

                
    
                  
  • F
    Find
    Ground the work in live sources, not stale snippets.
    • 
                  
  • O
    Observe
    Probe which beliefs survive a real test.
    • 
                  
  • R
    Recombine
    Blend the verified parts behind a tier firewall.
    • 
                  
  • G
    Grill
    A different model family attacks it, trying to break it.
    • 
                  
  • E
    Execute
    Build, run, prove. Done means ran, or it is not done.
    • 
                  
  • R
    Retain
    Persist the wins and the failures for the next run.
    •
